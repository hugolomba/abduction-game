GAME INSTRUCTIONS

You are a flying saucer pilot passing through a small planet called Earth.

Your objective is to score points by abducting the creatures you encounter, while avoiding obstacles that can damage or destroy you.

Use the ARROW KEYS to move around

Use the key SPACE to release the abduction beam

This planet is home to a variety of creatures, and each one gives a different score when abducted. But beware — the inhabitants also create dangerous objects. If you abduct those, they will lower your score!
